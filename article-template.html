This is where your article begins. The first letter gets a decorative drop-cap automatically. Write freely — this space has no word count, no performance pressure, just your thoughts finding their shape on the page.

You can write as many paragraphs as you like. The font is EB Garamond, which makes long reading feel comfortable and literary. For emphasis use em tags, and for strong points use strong tags — they'll render in pink and peach respectively.

A section heading

Use h2 for major sections of a long piece. It renders in soft pink. You can also add a blockquote like this:

"Something worth pulling out. A line from a paper. A thought that crystallised. A moment that deserved its own space."

For inline math you can use a span with class "math": the equation E = mc² sits naturally in text. For display math, use the math-block class:

∇ × B = μ₀J + μ₀ε₀ ∂E/∂t

And then carry on writing. The universe is large and there is plenty of room for your thoughts here. ✦
